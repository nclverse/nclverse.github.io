NCLVerse

Your education is now in your hands.

NCLVerse is a digital universe of apps that covers the whole spectrum of academics. It's put together fully using Clojure, a functional programming language. NCLVerse is currently in it's incubation phase and apps will be revealed one by one pretty soon.
